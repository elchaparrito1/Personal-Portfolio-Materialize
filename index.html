Gem Magic

Gem Magic is a simple click game utilizing the fundamentals of Jquery and vanilla Javascript. Clicking on a gem will add points your total score. You can win Gem Magic by matching your total score to the target number. The value of each gem is hidden until clicked upon. Choose wisely, and victory is yours.

Train Schedule

A simple app utilizing firebase to input parameters for a schedule. The user inputs the train name, destination, time, and frequency. Firebase is then used to output this information in the form of a schedule, and calculate the next train arrival and how many minutes away it is according to the current time.
